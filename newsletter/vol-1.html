03
Framework Correction

The Rule of 60 Clarification

Real talk: we need to correct something about the "Rule of 60." It's not just 60% profit margins — it's combined growth rate + profit margin = 60%. This framework helps AI-powered startups understand sustainable scaling.

  • The Math30% growth + 30% profit margin = Rule of 60. Or 40% growth + 20% profit. The balance matters more than hitting one extreme.
  • Why It WorksAI-powered businesses can achieve both high growth and high margins because their marginal costs are dramatically lower than traditional businesses.
  • The StandardThis becomes the new benchmark for lean, AI-enabled startups that can scale without massive capital requirements.

Appian Went Spec-Driven

Appian's move to spec-driven development this week signals something bigger: we're transitioning from "code-first" to "intent-first" business applications. When enterprise software starts generating itself from business requirements, everything changes.

  • The TechnologySpec-driven architecture means business analysts can define what they want, and the system generates the implementation automatically.
